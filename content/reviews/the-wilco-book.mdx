Wilco is one of those rare bands that rewards deep listening—every album reveals new layers the more time you spend with it. The Wilco Book is the physical embodiment of that philosophy: a richly designed, visually stunning document of the band's creative world. I have always been drawn to artists who take their craft seriously enough to question it, and Wilco has built an entire career on that kind of restless inquiry. This book belongs on the shelf of anyone who has ever felt that a piece of music—a chord, a lyric, a moment of noise—reached inside them and rearranged something.
